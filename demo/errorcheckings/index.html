Circus Modal View

Explanations

There were 2 things that happened in this tour

First thing: The modal for the 'circus' picture was not showed at the beginning. When you clicked on the 'circus' icon, FlexTourJS loops until the modal is sucessfully rendered without freezing the UI thread, then it starts rendering its components. This ensures the success of the tour.

Second thing: Similarly for the 'cake' picture, the difference is that the modal for the 'cake' never shows up. FlexTourJS also looped to find the modal as the next target, after certain amount of trials, it decided that the modal does NOT exist and move on to the next step.
